About me

In a nutshell

I am a self-taught designer currently living and working in Vienna, Austria.

My current and past professional roles include entrepreneurship educator, start-up founder, consultant, facilitator and - most importantly - designer.

I am obsessed with creativity. Therefore I initiated a project to explore this concept further and share back my findings through various formats, events and products.

If I could share one tip with fellow designers and creators it would be to keep a journal for your thoughts and ideas.
